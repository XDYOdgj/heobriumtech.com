JUXYES Wax Melt Warmer Liners

Enhance your wax melting experience with our durable and leakproof wax melt warmer liners. With their reinforced cardboard and protective coating, they are both reusable and easy to clean, making them a smart choice for everyday use.

  • Leakproof design ensures no mess, even with electric wax warmers
  • Durable cardboard with a protective coating for long-lasting use
  • Easy to change scents or fragrance oils multiple times a day
  • Adjustable size by gathering edges for a custom fit
  • Perfect for use with wax melt warmers, plug-in warmers, and candle burners

Why You’ll Love Them

  • Simple replacement keeps your waxing routine organized and clean
